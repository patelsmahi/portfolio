User Interface Challenges

As part of developing my UI/UX skills, I’ve taken on a variety of UI challenges to explore new techniques and push my creativity. These challenges gave me the opportunity to experiment with different layouts, visual styles, and interaction patterns while strengthening my design fundamentals. Along the way, I also sought feedback from peers, which helped me refine my work and approach each project with a more critical eye. Overall, these exercises have been an important part of my growth as a designer and have inspired me to keep exploring fresh ideas and innovative solutions.

Role: UI/UX Designer

Works

The Brunchary

The Brunchary is a hypothetical brunch restaurant for which I designed a mobile application. The app allows users to browse the menu, explore categories, and place orders directly from their phones. My focus was on creating a clean, intuitive interface that balances visual appeal with usability.

Key Design Highlights:

  • Designed clear navigation through menu categories (Small Eats, Brunch, Breakfast, Cocktails).
  • Showcased menu items with high-quality imagery and transparent pricing.
  • Built a seamless ordering flow to reduce friction for users.
  • Maintained a simple, consistent layout for easy readability and quick decisions.

Habit Tracker App

A minimalist mobile app designed for tracking daily habits and routines, emphasizing customization, simplicity, cleanliness, and motivation.

Key Design Highlights:

  • Focus on a minimalist visual style to reduce distractions and increase usability.
  • Allows users to customize categories to fit personal goals.
  • Features clear progress indicators for habit completion and consistency tracking.
  • Ensures lightweight and approachable layouts to encourage frequent engagement.

RooBeats

A local music discovery app for students at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. It helps users find live music events, explore local bands, and connect with the music scene, designed with a modern, simple, and engaging interface for a student audience.

Key Design Highlights:

  • Allows users to browse upcoming live music events around UNC.
  • Each band has a dedicated profile page with information about their music and background.
  • Designed to build community by strengthening connections between students and local musicians.
  • Features a clean, modern design appealing to a student audience and encouraging frequent use.

Check-Out Screen

This project focuses on designing an efficient and user-friendly checkout flow for a café ordering app, aiming for quick orders with minimal friction and a clean, inviting interface that reflects the café's atmosphere.

Key Design Highlights:

  • The checkout process was streamlined to ensure that customers can place an order in just a few quick steps.
  • The modern, minimalist design mirrors the café's brand identity, creating consistency from menu browsing to checkout.

The Democrat & Chronicle Mobile Design

This UI challenge focused on creating a mobile design for the Democrat & Chronicle magazine website, with the primary goal of conveying brand identity while ensuring a clean, consistent, modern, and user-friendly interface.

Key Design Highlights:

  • The design was structured to strongly reflect the Democrat & Chronicle's brand identity on mobile platforms.
  • A consistent visual style was maintained across pages to ensure clarity and familiarity for users.
  • The user experience prioritized accessibility and ease of use, allowing readers to focus on content without distractions.
  • The layout emphasized readability, with a modern design that minimized clutter.
