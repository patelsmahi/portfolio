About Me!

Bio

Hello! I’m Mahi Patel, a senior at UNC Chapel Hill majoring in Information Science with a minor in Media and Journalism. I’m in my final year of university, where I’ve shaped my studies around my passion for UI/UX design.

My interest in UI/UX grew from my love of both art and technology. While design can be creative, I see UI/UX as more than just an art form — it’s a bridge that connects users with technology in ways that are practical, usable, and accessible. I enjoy the challenge of creating interfaces that are not only visually clean but also meaningful and intuitive for the people who use them.

Outside of my studies, I work as an IT Support Technician at Oasis IT Support, where I continue to strengthen my technical skills and learn how people engage with technology. This balance of creative design and technical problem-solving shapes the way I approach my work.

In my free time, I enjoy baking and taking long scenic walks, which help me slow down, notice details, and find inspiration in everyday life.
